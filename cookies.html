Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Bright Study Hub uses cookies and similar technologies.

1. What cookies are

Cookies are small browser files. Similar technologies such as local storage may remember choices or help measure website use.

2. Necessary technologies

A necessary local-storage preference may remember your cookie selection so the banner is not displayed on every page.

3. Analytics

If enabled, analytics may help us understand useful resources, devices, errors and referral sources. Optional analytics should activate only after consent where required.

4. Your choices

Choose Accept all for optional analytics or Necessary only to keep them disabled. Clear this site’s browser data to reset the choice.

5. Third parties

Embedded learning tools, videos, analytics or links may use technologies under their own policies.

6. Contact

Cookie questions may be sent to privacy@brightstudyhub.com.
