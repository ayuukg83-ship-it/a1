Last updated: July 19, 2026

Bright Study Hub provides general educational information and study-skills resources.

Content is not a substitute for instruction, tutoring, educational assessment, disability support, counselling, medical care or advice from a qualified professional familiar with the student.

Study methods may support planning and practice, but we do not promise grades, examination results, admission, scholarships, certification or career outcomes.

We aim for clear information, but curricula, examination rules, institutional policies and digital tools change. Verify current requirements with the relevant school, teacher, university or examination authority.

Students must follow applicable rules for assignments, examinations, collaboration, citation and AI use. Our resources should not be used to misrepresent authorship or evade assessment requirements.

Study advice should be adapted to health, disability and learning needs. Seek appropriate professional or institutional support when needed. The website is not an emergency service.
