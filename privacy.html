Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

This policy explains how Bright Study Hub collects, uses and protects information when you browse, subscribe or contact us.

1. Information we collect

We may collect your name, email, messages and subscription choices. With consent, technical data may include visited pages, device type, approximate location, referral source and cookie identifiers.

2. How we use information

We use information to answer enquiries, deliver requested updates, improve resources, secure the site and meet legal duties. Marketing is sent only where permitted and includes an unsubscribe option.

3. Students and children

Our general resources may interest younger learners, but accounts, submissions and direct communications are intended for users able to provide valid consent or acting with appropriate parent, guardian or school authorisation. We do not knowingly request unnecessary sensitive student data.

4. Cookies and analytics

Optional analytics remain disabled until consent where required. Necessary local storage may remember your cookie choice.

5. Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Limited data may be shared with hosting, email, analytics and professional providers under appropriate obligations, or where legally required.

6. Retention and security

Information is retained only as reasonably necessary and protected using reasonable safeguards, although no online service is completely secure.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection or consent withdrawal at privacy@brightstudyhub.com.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@brightstudyhub.com. Identity or guardian verification may be required.
