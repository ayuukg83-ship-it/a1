Common questions

Use resources effectively

Which study method is best?

No method is universally best. Match the strategy to the subject, task and way your knowledge will be assessed.

How long should a study session be?

Use a duration you can sustain with genuine focus. Short breaks and clear goals usually matter more than a perfect timer.

Can these resources guarantee higher grades?

No. They support planning and practice, but results depend on many factors including effort, teaching, assessment and individual circumstances.
